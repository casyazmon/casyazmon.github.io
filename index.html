About me

Akap Azmon is computer programmer and a freelancer who builds software products and and provide technological solutions to facilate, better and change lives of people around the world. He was born in the North West region of Cameroon precisely in Bamenda on the 08 of March 1997. He started primary school at age of four where he was got his First School Leaving Certificate (FSLC). He continue thereafter to secondary and then high school in GBHS Down town Bamenda where obtained the Government Common Entrance Avance Level Certification (GCE A level) in science ..Mathematics, Physics , Chemistry, Biology and ICT. He later went to Institute University of Technology (University of Dschange), Cameroon in 2014 and Studied Computer Engineering where he obtained a:
